Team Members

Mack McGee (Owner)
Mack holds a master’s degree in exercise physiology from University at Buffalo, SUNY, and is the founder of Mack’s Old Iron Gym. He has coached competitive athletes for the past twenty years, some who have won national titles in olympic or power lifting. His interests are in both health and fitness, nutrition, and competitive sports. He has authored or coauthored multiple papers on health, fitness, and exercise, and continues to guest lecture at the State University of New York. He is a military veteran and volunteers his time to support local veteran organizations.

Deb Dory (Exercise Coach)
Deb has a Bachelor of Science in Exercise and Sports Science from Temple University in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. She is a certified personal trainer and sports nutrition coach through NASM. This is her seventh year as a trainer. Her specialties include track and field sports.

Brian Smith (Exercise Coach)
Brian holds a bachelor’s degree in exercise science from the University of West Alabama. He is a certified ISSA personal trainer with four years of experience. His interests include functional exercise, adaptive fitness, and sports specific exercise – especially football and baseball.

Sam Stone (Muscle Development Coach)
Sam has over 15 years in the fitness industry as a bodybuilder, fitness, and powerlifting coach. He has a master’s degree in biomechanics and sports physiotherapy from the University of Rhode Island. He has trained NPC/IFBB athletes and individuals who just want to improve their health and fitness.

Nigel Turner (Strength Development Coach)
Nigel is a competitive powerlifter who ranks nationally in the top ten percent of his weight class. He is a National Champion Powerlifter and has trained other powerlifters who have won state or regional championships. Nigel is a certified physical therapist and started competing in powerlifting in high school.
